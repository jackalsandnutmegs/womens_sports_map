About this map

This map exists to help people discover and support women’s football and rugby in their local area. All locations are club grounds or stadiums that clubs themselves have published or posted on social media or their websites.

If you represent a club and would like your entry updated or removed, please contact: jackals.and.nutmegs@gmail.com.
